Support Us!

The Falcons are always looking for supporters to assist them in making their program successful. Supporters are key for us to move further with our team and spread STEM awareness to others in the area.

Members

We love having new people work with us in our programs, regardless of skill level. Not only do new members become part of a large team, they learn specific STEM related skills, and also get to see the business side of running our FRC team. Students will also participate in our outreach program, by spreading interest to younger children, teaching kids how to work with robots, and maintaining the attributes of FIRST. If you are interested, you can fill out the sign up form or email us at contact@team4099.com.

Mentors

What better way to teach kids about STEM than from experts themselves? That's why we are constantly looking for mentors to help our team learn the process of making a robot, from the fundamentals to the end game. We want our mentors to guide our students, while still maintaining the student-run aspect of our team. If you are interested in helping our team and being a mentor, email us at contact@team4099.com.

Sponsors

Our sponsors help us saty afloat in the always competitive FIRST robotics competition. Their support can range from monetary donations, to providing us with tools and materials, and even mentorship to our team. If your company is willing to provide us with money, parts, tools, or anything else, feel free to contact us at contact@team4099.com.
