Join Now!

If you are interested in joining Team 4099, fill out the form below!

The costs associated with taking part in the FIRST Robotics Competition are very high - high enough that even with the help of our corporate sponsors, we cannot fill our entire budget. For this reason, Team 4099 has calculated that a $115 team fee is necessary. This money will be used to pay for team registration, tools and equipment, and robot parts. This money will not be used to pay team members or parents or anything like that. All expenditures by the team will be made public at the end of the school year, or at the request of any members or parents.

If this team fee is a burden on you and your family, or you are only tentatively interested in the team, or feel uncomfortable with paying the fee for any reason, please talk to the team captains during a team meeting or email us at contact@team4099.com and we will figure out an alternative method for you to contribute to the team.
